Dining with the Distinguished

This is a night of banquet style food and networking between ambitious students and business professionals, taking place on Friday, December 4th at Rutgers around 6:30pm. Any student interested in career advice can attend for the opportunity to network with distinguished representatives from a variety of companies. Last year, we had 200 students and 40 professionals in attendance.

Where: Rutgers New Brunswick, Busch Student Center, Multipurpose Room

When: Friday, December 4th, 2015 6:30PM - Doors open at 6:00PM
